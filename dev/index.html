GridAnalysis.UCED — Type
UCED <: DayAhead

Structure with two DA systems stored (system_uc and system_ed). One represents an Unit Commitment (UC) used to fix binary variables, the other an Economic Dispatch (ED) defined by template template_ed. Each model needs its own solver (solver_uc and solver_ed). system_ed and the resulting problem are defined as a heuristic to calculate DA energy prices.
